About us

Here at Eco-Guard, we wanted to help people be more eco-friendly.

But we realized how difficult it is to be eco-friendly, especially when shopping online.

We wondered, what if we could make it simple for people to choose better products, at similar prices, and with lesser environmental impact?

And what if we helped people protect the environment before recycling a product even becomes an issue?

Thus and thus, Eco-Guard, your eco-advisor was born.

Our EcoRating

The Eco-Rating is our algorithm for calculating the environmental impact of a product.

As our product develops, so does our algorithm.

We believe that to be effective, this needs to be as transparent as possible. Head over to our website to find out more.
