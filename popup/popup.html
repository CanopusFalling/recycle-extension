Who are we?

Here at Eco-Guard, we wanted to help people to be more eco-friendly.

We realised how difficult it is to be eco-friendly, especially when shopping online.

So we thought, what if we could make it simple for people to choose better products, at similar prices, with less environmental impact

And, what if, we helped people change their habits, before recycling it, even becomes an issue.

So, Eco-Guard, your personal Eco-Advisor was born.

What is the Eco-Rating?

Our Eco-Rating is our algorithm for calculating the environmental impact of a product.

As our product develops, so does our algorithm.

We believe that to be effective, this needs to be as transparent as possible.
